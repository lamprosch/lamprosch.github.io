Bio

Lampros Chantzis is an audio engineer and producer from Patras, Greece. He studied Electrical and Computer Engineering and served as part of the Audio & Acoustics Technology Group at the University of Patras, where he worked on spatial audio, upmixing algorithms, and psychoacoustics. This technical background continues to inform his approach to sound, especially in projects that involve imaging, space, and listener perception. Alongside his engineering work, Lampros has been a guitarist for more than twenty years, writing and performing original music shaped by indie, shoegaze, and post‑rock influences. His production style reflects that musical world — guitar‑focused textures, atmosphere, and a hybrid analog‑digital workflow that balances precision with character. He has worked as a recording engineer in studios in Copenhagen and contributed at Roskilde Festival in 2022 and 2023. He is currently working as a sound engineer in Barcelona, collaborating with local artists and taking on projects both on technical research and practical audio production, and occasionally writing about music and sound when the subject feels worth exploring.
